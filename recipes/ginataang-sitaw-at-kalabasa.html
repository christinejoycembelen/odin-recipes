Ginataang Sitaw at Kalabasa

Description

Calabaza squash and string beans cooked in coconut milk.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp or shrimp heads (you can also use pork)
  • Coconut milk
  • Squash
  • Long beans
  • Garlic and onion
  • Chili
  • Fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Saute the aromatics. Saute onion and garlic and cook until softened. Add fish sauce and continue to cook for at least one minute while stirring.
  2. Add coconut milk and chili peppers. If you want the dish to have a thicker broth, boil it for 3 to 5 minutes on low heat. Since i like mine soup-y, I add the squash around the same time so it can cook faster.
  3. Add squash and long beans. Cook the squash for 3 to 5 minutes or until fork tender but not falling apart. Add the long beans next and cook for 3 to 5 minutes as well or until tender-crisp.
  4. Add shrimp or shrimp heads. Add shrimp and continue to cook for about 5 minutes or until the color changes.
  5. Season and serve. Season with salt and pepper to taste. I also season mine with sugar just in case it's not sweet enough for me. Serve and eat with hot steamed rice.
