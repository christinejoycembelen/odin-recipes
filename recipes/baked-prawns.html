Baked Prawns

Description

Baked butterflied prawns with a butter-mayo-cheese filling perfect as an appetizer or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs prawns
  • 1/4 C butter
  • 1/4 C mayo
  • 1 C grated cheese
  • 7 garlic cloves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • calamansi optional

Steps

  1. Combine the ingredients for the filling. In a bowl, add softened butter, mayo, grated cheese, minced garlic, and salt and pepper. Mix well to combine and place in the refrigerator until ready to use.
  2. Butterfly the shrimp. Cut the shell with kitchen scissors and devein the shrimp using a toothpick. Slice the meat at the center, but do not cut all the way through. Open up the shrimp and lay it flat. Finally, arrange the shrimps on a baking sheet lined with aluminum foil or parchment paper. Optional: Remove the heads of the shrimps. You can use these for stocks or to add flavor to other side dishes.
  3. Add filling to shrimps. Add a spoonful of the butter-mayo-cheese mixture to each butterflied shrimp. Optional: Sprinkle quick-melt cheese on top for more cheesiness.
  4. Bake. Bake the shrimp in a preheated oven at 400Â°F for 12 minutes.
  5. Serve. Baste the shrimp with the melted butter from the filling and transfer to a plate. Squeeze calamansi on the shrimp and serve while still warm.
